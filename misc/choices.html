AI-Human Collaboration & The Job Market

Choices:

  • Gig-economy self-sustaining jobs
  • Contact-to-contract workforce
  • Personal choice & freedom on AI-Human collaboration within a job sector.

The increased use of AI within business has also given rise to the "Gig Economy" which is a freely available choice for you to pursue. These jobs usually are short-term, freelancer-like jobs where you are independent, and are paid on a contract-to-contract basis. This can be a variety of one-off jobs to short temporary hires for months at a time. The most common Gig Economy jobs you might know can include a lot of food delivery services such as Uber, or even hotel apps like AirBnB. Other independent self-sustaining jobs arise in the case where a service is in short supply due to high consumer demand. Overall, it's inevitable that AI will drastically change how a workforce operates. In fact, a survey of 1,075 companies in 12 industries found that the more of AI-Human collaboration principles companies adopted (principles such as standard business practices, employee involvement, cultivating and re-imagining employee skills, etc) found an increased overall performance in terms of speed, cost savings, revenues, or other operational measures. A big disadvantage AI has is that what comes naturally to people (making a joke, for example) can be tricky for machines, and what’s straightforward for machines (analyzing gigabytes of data) remains virtually impossible for humans.


I will now leave you with this closing statement: "While AI will radically alter how work gets done and who does it, the technology’s larger impact will be in complementing and augmenting human capabilities, not replacing them."
